About

Cyberpunk: Edgerunners ( Japanese :サイバーパンク エッジランナーズ, Hepburn : Saibāpanku Edjirannāzu ) is a cyberpunk original internet animation (ONA) miniseriesbased on the video game Cyberpunk 2077 by Polish studio CD Projekt Red . The series is animated by Japanese animation studio Trigger under CD Projekt's supervision and premiered on Netflix in September 2022. Set in the Cyberpunk universecreated by Mike Pondsmith , the anime serves as a prequel to the game and takes place approximately one year before the events of Cyberpunk 2077 .

Character

Lucyna Kushinada

A young and mysterious netrunner who becomes romantically involved with David and introduces him to the criminal underworld of Night City. She has a particular hatred towards Arasaka and dreams of traveling to the Moon.

David Martinez

A Latino American teenager who is a top student at the prestigious Arasaka Academy. Because he comes from a very poor family, he is relentlessly bullied by his classmates and feels like he does not belong at school.

Rebecca

A young and trigger-happy edgerunner and a member of Maine's crew. She is also Pilar's younger sister.

Dorio

Marie Westbrook (English) Maine's girlfriend and second-in-command.

Maine

A veteran edgerunner who commands his own crew. He is one of Gloria's clients and allows David to join the crew under his guidance.

Faraday

A fixer who works for Militech. He has a business relationship with Maine's crew and often hires them to conduct jobs that usually involve them performing corporate espionage against Arasaka.
